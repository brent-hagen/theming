opposite world
==============

:: Description
There is a hypothetical world (maybe a parallel universe or a fantasy land) where
some or several laws of nature (or suchlike aspects of reality) are reversed.

:: Parents
speculative reality

:: Examples
The Enterprise got sucked into just such a universe in tas2x06 "The Counter-
Clock Incident".


reverse aging
=============

:: Description
There is a character who starts life as old and then becomes younger as time passes.

:: Parents
speculative natural process
